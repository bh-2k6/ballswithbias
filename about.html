About

Balls With Bias is an independent cricket writing platform. It does not pretend to be neutral, balanced, or sanitised for consensus. The goal here is simple: say what most coverage avoids.

Cricket discourse often hides behind diplomacy - administrators, boards, and even players are treated with excessive caution. This site exists to challenge that culture, especially when politics, power structures, and selective outrage shape the game more than performances on the field.
